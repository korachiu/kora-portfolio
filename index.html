About

Hello There! My name is Kora, and I am a graphic designer and illustrator based in Los Angeles, currently pursuing a BFA in Graphic Design at California Institute of the Arts (CalArts). I currently freelance for the advertising company Illuminista and was previously hired as a Teaching Assistant at CalArts. I believe that great design stems from strong methodology, high personal standards, and a simple love for puzzles. When I’m not designing, I’m making silly duck drawings, building miniature models, listening to music, or watching movies!
